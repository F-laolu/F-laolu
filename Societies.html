SOCIETIES

Developers Society

This society involves young developers who enjoy making programs. It includes various challenges ranging from:

- Hackthebox (This year was the cryptography challenge).
- Python basics workshop for individuals with the desire to learn python.

Presently, as a society, we are running a session on IBM Master the Mainframe competition for the Digital Horizon's week.

I have spent 4-hours of my CPD in this society.


CERT Mentor Session

This is a mentor session which runs every once a week for an hour. It is led by 2nd and final year students to help freshers like myself gain deeper understanding of the course.
They also share past experiences to enable 1st years learn from mistakes and what they did well.

Joining in this session was one of the first steps to me settling down in to university life.

I have spent 3-hours of my CPD in this session


Course Rep

As an enthusiastic student, applying for the course rep position was something i was interested in.

As a course rep, i am expected to:

- Listen to what my course mates have to say about the course

- Present my suggestions to the NTSU team.

These roles are discussed during every meetings.

I have spent 7-hours of my CPD in the meetings
